Welcome to the classic Tetris game! Developed with the Godot engine, this version lets you enjoy the block-stacking fun anytime, anywhere.

Tetris is the iconic puzzle game that tests players' spatial reasoning and reaction time, created by Russian programmer Alexey Pajitnov in 1984.

Game rules are simple: control the falling tetromino pieces and arrange them to create complete horizontal lines that clear from the board. As the game progresses, pieces fall faster, increasing the challenge.

How to Play Tetris

  • Use arrow keys or WASD to move and rotate pieces
  • Complete horizontal lines to clear them and score points
  • Avoid letting the stack reach the top of the playing field
  • Plan ahead to create spaces for upcoming pieces
